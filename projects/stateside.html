Stateside Website

Stateside is a premier nearshore talent company, that connects businesses with top-tier professionals. they enable companies to access exceptional talent and optimize their operations for success.

I had the pleasure to work on the Art Direction, UX/UI Designer for their website redesign project. My role involved overseeing the entire design process, from concept ideation to final implementation. By implementing Atomic Design principles, I was able to create a comprehensive library of components and styles that unified the company's digital presence across various platforms.

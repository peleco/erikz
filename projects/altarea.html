Altarea website

Altarea is a company dedicated to the development of shopping centers in France, they needed a website that could be declined to all their centers, starting with Qwartz as pilot.

I participate in the creation of the fully responsive website and was in charge of the art direction, interactions and the initials ui designs.

The complex part was that they needed an adaptable style guide system that could be declined for all the shopping centers with their different identities. It was a challenge to do something with personality without having the feeling of a declined template.

Everything was thought as a design system were every component could be adapted to differents types of content, so with just setting some parameters of a module we could have many declinations and solve different layouts.
